A few years ago I hit a psychological rough patch.
I felt alone and ashamed for being weak, when everyone else seemed to be doing fine under similar circumstances.
As I started to talk about my struggles to people around me, I realized that everyone is in pain in some form or the other.
I wondered; if pain is universal, why then do we feel so alone and isolated?

We are all suffering from something. Normality is relative.

I want the boundaries of sanity to be stretched a little wider, for psychological unbalances to be a little less taboo and to challenge what we believe to be 'socially acceptable'.

I write, draw, build and do numerous other things. Through these means I wish to provoke an array of emotions.
My hope is that everyone may find something to relate to.

So enjoy and remember what the Cat in Alice in wonderland said: 'we're all mad here'.
