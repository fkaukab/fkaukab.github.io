Elogy of a sister

03.04.2012

Depuis que t'es parti
I feel so empty.
I feel a void,
Where before there was your yapping.
To fill the space,
I do a lot of napping.

I turn on the TV,
Just so I can ignore it.
Even my CV,
Just doesn't cut it.

I feel the need to change my expression,
But no one whom to show it.
From empathetic,
Desperate,
To utter boredom.

Only realizing too late!
If there was a point,
Had I just missed it?
